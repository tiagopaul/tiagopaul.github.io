Tiago Paúl

PhD Student in Economics at the London School of Economics

I am a second-year PhD student in Economics at the London School of Economics (LSE), under the supervision of Ricardo Reis. My main research interests are in structural and empirical macroeconomics, particularly in topics relating to fiscal policy and labour markets.

I was previously a pre-doctoral research assistant working at the LSE's Centre for Macroeconomics. I worked for Ethan Ilzetzki on projects in fiscal policy and innovation, and for Liliana Varela in international macro-finance.

I obtained my MSc in Economics also from the London School of Economics, and my BSc in Economics from Nova School of Business and Economics.

You can contact me at t.brinkmann-torres@lse.ac.uk.
